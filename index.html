Our Story

Henderson Red Angus

Henderson Red Angus is a family story that spans generations. Frank and Karen Henderson ran commercial cattle in North Texas starting around 1980, and Toby got his first cows at about age 10 — ranching has always been in the blood.

For years Frank and Toby ran their commercial herds side by side, owning their cattle separately but working the land together. Around 2010-2011 they made it official — joining forces, splitting everything equally, and founding Henderson Red Angus as a registered Red Angus seedstock operation.

Today Frank, Karen, Toby, and Amy run the operation together, focused on producing performance-tested Red Angus bulls and females that work in the real world — easy fleshing, structurally sound, and proven by real world data. As a proud RA Brown Ranch cooperator, select Henderson Red Angus bulls are developed and sold through their program — while additional bulls are developed on grass here at the ranch and sold private treaty direct from Seymour.
